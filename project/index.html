The abbreviation of MEG is Momtaz Uddin Education Group. Mr Momtaz Uddin is an education minded social worker and simple person. According to the name of Mr Momtaz Uddin, his eldest son Principal Nasir Uddin established The first Business Management College in Bangladesh under Bangladesh Technical Education Board named as Momtaz Uddin Business Management College. After this to expand education, he takes step to establish several other institutes.
